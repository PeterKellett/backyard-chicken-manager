Feeding Time - Set-up Instructions

IMPORTANT! To get started we need to know the total amount of food and water in your feeders when you record your first feed. For example, if there is 1kg in the feeder from the previous feed and you then add 2kg, put 3kg in the field below. The same principal applies to water.

For all subsequent feeds, you will need to enter the amount of food or water remaining from the previous feed, plus the amount added.

PLEASE NOTE: If you scatter your food as opposed to using a feeder, enter 0 below and in the "Amount of Food Remaining" on subsequent feeds, unless you have a means of estimating the amount.

In addition to specific feed types, you should also record the amount of food scraps given.

Feeding Time - Page Instructions

Each time you feed your birds, complete this page.

IMPORTANT! The first time you record a feed, it is vital that you state the total amount of food and water in the feeders. For subsequent feeds, please indicate the amount of food and water remaining in the feeders after the last feed and the amount added. This allows us to calculate the average amount of food and water consumed by each bird which can be viewed in the "Reports" section.

  • Date: Add the feeding date.
  • Select Flock: Select which flock was fed (only displays if you have multiple flocks. If you have several flocks, you can add another feeding after you click save).
  • Select Food: This is the quantity of full trays.
  • Amount of Food Remaining: Amount of feed remaining in the feeder prior to adding more.
  • Amount of Food Added: Amount of food added to the feeder.
  • Amount of Water Remaining: Amount of water remaining in the feeder prior to adding more.
  • Amount of Water Added: Amount of water added to the feeder.
  • Notes: If you have any notes about the collection, add them here.
  • Photos: If you have any photos from the collection, upload them here.
  • Save Button: Click this button when finished.
  • Cancel Button: Click this button to clear all data entered.
